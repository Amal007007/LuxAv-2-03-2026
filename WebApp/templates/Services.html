Our Story

LuxAv is a premium audio-visual destination established in 2004 in Kochi, Kerala, built to serve enthusiasts who value true high-fidelity sound and cinematic visual experiences. From the beginning, our mission has been simple: deliver world-class home entertainment solutions backed by expertise, authenticity, and long-term customer support.

Over the years, LuxAV has evolved into a trusted name for high-end audio and video gear, offering a carefully curated range of products including reference headphones, IEMs, amplifiers, AV receivers, loudspeakers, high-resolution displays, projectors, and immersive home-theatre systems supporting Dolby Atmos, DTS:X, and Hi-Res Audio.
